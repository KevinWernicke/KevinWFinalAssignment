Media Player

New to the XTOUCH line comes XTOUCH Media Player with the power of accessing your music library on any surface. For the times when you can't touch your phone to change a song XTOUCH Media Player allows you to easily setup the virtual buttons you need to browse through your songs and play them. XTOUCH Media Player make every surface rock and roll!

Iphone/Ipad

XTOUCH software right now is only supported on Iphone and Ipad Devices. XTOUCH is supported by Apple because the software needs their highly advanced built-in microphone to hear for any user taps on surfaces.

Features

XTOUCH Media Player allows anyone to have their music library at the tap of a surface. With many different icons to choose from you make the choice of the virtual buttons you want tappable access to. If you get lost don't worry there is an easy access tutorial within the app to help guide you along the way.
